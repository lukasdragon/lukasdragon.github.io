Project Kreuthil

Project Kreuthil is a collabrative complete overhaul and expansion mod for minecraft.

Development

The development of the mod began as a way to teach TheLizardKing45 how to program but has since
grown to its current state. Active development of the mod is currently temporarily paused, however, should you
decide to contribute, they are greatly welcome as we will be resuming development shortly.

Open-Source

Project Kreuthil is licensed under the MIT license which is short and permissive.
Basically, you can do whatever you want as long as you include the original
copyright and license notice in any copy of the software/source.
There are many variations of this license in use.
